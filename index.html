Yoga Wellness

Bali Mindfulness & Yoga Retreat

Hosted by Sarah Johnson
üìç Ubud, Bali
üóìÔ∏è 7 days
üë• 12 spots

Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Ubud's lush jungle while deepening your yoga practice and cultivating mindfulness. This 7-day retreat offers the perfect balance of structured yoga sessions, guided meditation, and free time to explore Bali's stunning landscapes and rich culture.

Wake up to the sounds of nature, practice yoga overlooking rice terraces, and nourish your body with organic, locally-sourced cuisine. Whether you're new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, this retreat will leave you feeling refreshed, centered, and deeply connected.

What's Included

  • 7 nights accommodation in a private villa
  • Daily yoga and meditation sessions (morning & evening)
  • All meals - organic, vegetarian cuisine
  • Guided nature walks and temple visits
  • Traditional Balinese healing ceremony
  • Airport transfers to/from retreat center
  • Welcome and farewell dinners

Daily Schedule

  • 6:30 AM - Morning meditation & pranayama
  • 7:30 AM - Vinyasa yoga flow
  • 9:00 AM - Healthy breakfast
  • 10:30 AM - 4:00 PM - Free time (workshops, spa, exploration)
  • 4:30 PM - Yin yoga & restorative practice
  • 6:00 PM - Dinner
  • 7:30 PM - Evening meditation or group sharing

Application Requirements

This retreat has limited spots and requires a brief application. We're looking for committed individuals who are ready to embrace the full experience.

  • Brief introduction about yourself and your yoga journey
  • What you hope to gain from this retreat
  • Any dietary restrictions or health considerations
  • $500 reservation fee (credited toward total cost)

About Your Host

Sarah Johnson is a 500-hour certified yoga instructor with over 10 years of teaching experience. She's led retreats across Southeast Asia and is passionate about creating transformative experiences that blend traditional yoga philosophy with modern mindfulness practices. Sarah's teaching style is nurturing, intuitive, and deeply grounding.
